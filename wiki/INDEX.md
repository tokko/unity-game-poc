## Curation rules

- **Update a page** whenever you discover something new about that topic.
- **Add a page** when a new topic doesn't fit an existing page; then add a row here.
- **Keep entries short** — one paragraph per fact, no duplicate prose across pages.
- **Date discoveries** with `<!-- YYYY-MM-DD -->` inline so stale facts are visible.
